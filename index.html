Our Story

At Wake & Bake, we believe in the magic of freshly baked goods. Since 2020, we've been crafting artisan bread and pastries using traditional techniques and the finest locally-sourced ingredients.

Every morning, our bakers start before dawn to ensure you get the freshest, most delicious baked goods possible. From our signature sourdough to our flaky croissants, each item is made with passion and expertise.
